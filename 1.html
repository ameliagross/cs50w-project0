Toasted Coconut French Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 Chino Valley Ranchers Pasture Raised Organic Egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup toasted coconut almond milk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla coconut collagen peptides (vanilla or coconut protein works well too)
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 2 slices multigrain bread
  • ¼ cup shredded unsweetened coconut
  • ¼ cup blueberries
  • ⅓ cup sliced strawberries
  • ½ sliced banana

Directions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the egg, vanilla, collagen peptides, and almond milk, then set aside
  2. In a skillet, melt coconut oil over medium heat.
  3. Dip bread one slice at a time into the mixture, soaking each side.
  4. Place bread onto preheated skillet and cook until golden brown on each side.
  5. Slice toast diagonally and serve topped with strawberries, blueberries, banana slices, coconut peanut butter, and shredded coconut! Enjoy!
